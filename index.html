About Falcons Nest

The Falcons Nest is more than just a school store - it's a hub of school spirit and a resource for all students at our high school. Established in 2010, the Falcons Nest has been providing essential school supplies, spirit wear, and other necessities to our student body.

üè´

Our mission is to make school life easier by providing convenient access to the supplies students need most, while also promoting school pride through our selection of Falcons merchandise. From pencils to sweatshirts, we've got what you need to succeed and show your school spirit!

The Falcons Nest is run by a dedicated team of student volunteers and staff advisors. This hands-on experience provides real-world business skills to our student workers, including inventory management, customer service, and financial literacy.

All proceeds from the Falcons Nest go directly back into student programs, funding activities, clubs, and events that enrich our school community.
