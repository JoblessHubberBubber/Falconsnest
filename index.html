About The Falcons Nest

Learn more about our school store and the team behind it

Our Mission

The Falcons Nest is operated by and for the students of Falcons High School. Our mission is to provide affordable school supplies and spirit wear while teaching students valuable business skills. All profits from the store go directly back to supporting student activities and clubs.

Store Hours

Monday - Friday: 7:30 AM - 8:15 AM and during lunch periods (11:00 AM - 1:00 PM)

Special extended hours during school events and parent-teacher conferences.

Location

We're located in Room 112, near the cafeteria entrance.

Meet Our Team

The Falcons Nest is proudly run by students in the Business Leadership class, with guidance from faculty advisors.

Our History

The Falcons Nest was established in 2010 as a small kiosk selling only pencils and notebooks. It has since grown into a full-service school store offering a wide range of products to meet the needs of our students.

Community Impact

Through your purchases, the Falcons Nest has been able to:

  • Fund 12 student clubs
  • Provide 25 scholarships to graduating seniors
  • Support the annual school carnival
  • Donate supplies to local elementary schools

Contact Us

Have questions or suggestions for products we should carry? Email us at falconsnest@falconshigh.edu or stop by during store hours.
